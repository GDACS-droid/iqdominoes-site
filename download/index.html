What’s New in v1.6

Already have the app? Here’s what you get when you update.

  • Post-game share image — capicúa, zapato, and match wins generate a shareable PNG for WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messages.
  • Live emoji reactions — six curated emojis (🔥 😂 🤔 👏 😱 🎉) float across the table during online matches.
  • Live queue counter — see how many players are searching right now while you wait for a match.
  • Daily Challenge + streak — one seeded game per day, with a days-played streak counter.
  • Block + Report players — both options on every opponent after a match.
  • Privacy opt-out toggle — turn off analytics and crash reports in Settings.
