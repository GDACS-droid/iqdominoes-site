Why players come back

🎴 Authentic double-nine play

All 55 tiles, real Cuban rules. Capicúa scores extra, shutouts (pollona) pay double — the way Tío taught it.

👥 True multiplayer

1v1, 2v2 teams, 4-player free-for-all, or solo against CPU opponents on three difficulty levels.

📤 Share your wins

Post-match recap images go straight to WhatsApp, Instagram, or Messages. Brag responsibly.

🔥 Live emoji reactions

React mid-game with a curated set of six emojis. No open chat, no harassment — just table talk.

🎯 Daily Challenge + streaks

Beat one seeded game every day. Track your days-played streak and keep the run alive.

🔒 Privacy-first

No ads during play, no real-money wagers, no pay-to-win. In-app opt-out for analytics, block/report on every player.
