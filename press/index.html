Boilerplate

IQ Dominoes is a free, multiplayer Cuban dominoes game for iOS and Android. Built by Cuban-American developers, it brings authentic double-nine rules — capicúa scoring, zapato (shutout) bonuses, and partner play — to mobile without ads during play, without real-money wagering, and without pay-to-win mechanics. Players match strangers online, set up private rooms with friends, or play offline against three difficulty levels of CPU opponents.

The version 1.6 release adds shareable post-game recap images, live emoji reactions during play, and a Daily Challenge with streak tracking. IQ Dominoes is published by Global Data and Consulting Solutions, LLC ("GDACS Games"), a Florida-registered limited liability company founded in 2021.

What's new in v1.6

  • Post-game share image — render a recap of the match, scores, and winning play to share to WhatsApp, Messages, or Instagram.
  • Live emoji reactions — tap a reaction during play; opponents see it on the table. No open chat.
  • Daily Challenge — a new seeded hand every day, with streak tracking.
  • Stability and polish — board layout no longer drifts when the hand ends; chain renders symmetrically in 2v2 mode.
